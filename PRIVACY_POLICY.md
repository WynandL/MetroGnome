# Privacy Policy for MetroGnome

**Effective date:** 2026-04-06

## Overview

MetroGnome ("the App") is a free metronome and rhythm training app for Android. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The App does not require account registration, login, or any personal information to function.

## Microphone Permission

The App requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable the optional **clap/tap detection** feature in the Rhythm Game. Audio is processed in real time on your device only — it is never recorded, saved, or transmitted anywhere.

You can use the App fully without granting microphone access by tapping the screen instead.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

- Device identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
- IP address and general location
- App usage information

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details:
[https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

To opt out of personalized advertising on your device, visit your device's **Settings → Privacy → Ads**.

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
